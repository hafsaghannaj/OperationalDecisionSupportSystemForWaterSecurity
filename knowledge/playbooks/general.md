# General Operational Playbook

## Elevated risk
- Increase water point chlorination checks in the flagged districts.
- Pre-position water purification tablets, oral rehydration salts, and field test kits.
- Send targeted hygiene and safe-storage messaging through local community health workers.

## Critical risk
- Start a same-week incident review across WASH, surveillance, and logistics teams.
- Deploy rapid water quality testing to the highest-risk settlements and transport corridors.
- Prepare surge staffing and referral capacity before cases rise above the district baseline.

## Field verification
- Confirm whether rainfall, flooding, and service disruptions are affecting shared water sources.
- Compare local clinic observations with the latest dashboard hotspot pattern before escalation.
- Document actions taken, unresolved gaps, and any request for regional support.
