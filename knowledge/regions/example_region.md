# Example Region Notes

## River delta context
- Prioritize embankment-adjacent unions where overflow and latrine washout happen after back-to-back rainfall weeks.
- Coordinate with boat-access teams because road access drops quickly once floodwater crosses the lowland connectors.

## Community messaging
- Use short Bangla voice or SMS prompts focused on chlorination, safe storage, and early care-seeking for watery diarrhea.
- Pair hygiene messaging with visible stock availability so the alert leads to action rather than risk fatigue.
